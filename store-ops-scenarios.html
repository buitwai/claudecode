Your Mission

Key Features Required

  • Barcode scanning with instant product lookup
  • Vehicle-specific part compatibility checking
  • Commercial account pricing and credit terms
  • Installation service scheduling
  • Warranty registration and lookup
  • Return processing with reason codes
  • Multi-tender payment processing

Create a modern POS interface for AutoZone stores:
